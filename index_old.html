What is 1/0?

A design studio where code, art, and humanity converge.

We craft evocative experiences with new technologies. Our process is about hiding the machine to reveal the feeling, leaving only a sense of wonder. We're not a production house. We're not just developers. We're illusionists of the digital kind — building experiences that blur the line between technology and magic.
